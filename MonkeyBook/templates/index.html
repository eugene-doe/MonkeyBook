What does it do?

Assuming that you are a monkey, you can:

  • create a profile on Monkeybook,
  • see your profile,
  • edit your details,
  • see a list of other monkeys,
  • add other monkeys as your friends,
  • set one of your friends as the best friend,
  • remove friends,
  • delete your profile (although why would you?).
